Meet Youngster

Pranit Chaulagain, born on November 9, 2006, is a young Nepali rapper and music artist known by his stage name, Youngster. His journey in music began at a young age, inspired by the struggles and stories around him, much like his idol Yama Buddha. Growing up, Pranit found his passion for music as a means to express himself and bring awareness to societal issues, much like how Yama Buddha used hip hop as a tool for social change.

Influenced by the deep messages in Yama Buddha's work, Pranit channels his thoughts and feelings into his music, with a unique style that blends creativity and conscious lyrics. As a young artist, he is determined to make his mark in the Nepali music industry, using his platform to inspire others and share stories of growth, resilience, and hope.

Pranit's music is not just about rapping; it is an extension of his thoughts, his voice, and his desire to contribute positively to the world, drawing inspiration from Yama Buddha's legacy of speaking truth through powerful, thought-provoking art.
